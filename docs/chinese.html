Technology技术

Contrib aims to build a universal “Contribution Graph”: a verified, distributed & immutable register of projects & who contributed to them. Contrib旨在建立一个通用的“贡献图”：一个经过验证的，分布式的和不可变的项目注册表，并为他们做出了贡献。 Such a system can answer the following question: Who contributed what, where?这样的系统可以回答以下问题：谁贡献什么，在哪里？ eg “Linus Torvalds created the Linux Kernel” or “Brian Chesky co-founded AirBnB in 2008”.例如“Linus Torvalds创建了Linux内核”或“Brian Chesky在2008年共同创立了AirBnB”。 Such a register can be of tremendous value to the creative professions in the Open Source and Startup communities.这样一个登记册对于开放源代码社区和创业社区的创意专业来说可能是巨大的价值。

Such a graph can be used to assess a project contributor's reputation, assign a fair equity split in early projects or even automatically compensate team members for the work they have done.这样的图表可用于评估项目贡献者的声誉，在早期项目中分配公平股权，甚至自动补偿团队成员进行的工作。 There are 3 main components required to build such a graph: People, Roles & Projects.构建这样的图形需要3个主要组件：人物，角色和项目。 Roles (eg “CTO”, “Designer”, “Developer”) form the edges linking together People & Project nodes in a graph.角色（例如“CTO”，“Designer”，“开发人员”）形成了将图表中的“人物”和“项目”节点连接在一起的边缘。 Project & Role descriptions are reached by voted consensus among the People participating in a project.参与项目的人员通过投票协商达成项目和角色描述。

Blockchain technology offers a couple of interesting properties appropriate for a project like CONTRIB. Blockchain技术提供了几个有趣的属性，适用于像CONTRIB这样的项目。 The following are the core characteristics CONTRIB requires to work: IMMUTABILITY, TRANSPARENCY and DECENTRALISATION.以下是CONTRIB要求工作的核心特征：不可执行性，透明度和分散性。

IMMUTABILITY is achieved by storing Roles as contracts or transactions in the blockchain.通过将角色作为合约或事务存储在块链中来实现不可实现性。 TRANSPARENCY is achieved through a voting process to define project descriptions and roles: all project members need to agree for changes to be made.透明度通过投票过程来实现，以定义项目描述和角色：所有项目成员需要同意进行更改。 The blockchain transaction history serves as a transparent representation of how a consensus was reached.块交易历史可以透明地表达如何达成共识。 DECENTRALISATION of identity verification is desirable long-term and can be achieved through collaboration with projects like Civic.身份验证的分散化是长期的，可以通过与像Civic这样的项目合作实现。

Roadmap路线图

Early July 2017 : Ideation and Community Feedback We will present the draft whitepaper & interact with the crypto community before finalising the final version that will be used for ICO. 早期的2017年7月：思维能力和社区反馈我们将介绍白皮书草案和与加密社区最终确定将被用于ICO最终版本之前进行互动。 This will present an opportunity for the larger crypto community to shape how the contribution graph will be built and how the ICO will be handled.这将为更大的加密社区提供一个机会，以形成如何构建贡献图，以及如何处理ICO。

Q3 2017 : MVP, Pre-Registration, Pre-Sale and ICO An MVP of the web interface will be made available where users can pre-register their CONTRIB accounts at no cost. 2017年第三季度：Web界面的MVP，预先登记，售前和ICO的MVP将提供，用户可以在没有成本预先登记自己的contrib账户。 Per-Sale for larger buyers will be organised through a Slack channel.大型买家的每次销售将通过Slack渠道进行组织。 After Public ICO token holders will be able to formally claim their accounts & use tokens to request formal IDV.公共ICO令牌持有人将能够正式申领账户并使用令牌申请正式的IDV。 In case of overfunding > 10M USD a fund will be set up to subsidise IDV for key members of the open source community.在资金超过1000万美元的情况下，将设立一个基金来为开源社区的主要成员补贴IDV。

Q4 2017 : Identity Verification Launch Token holders will be able to request Identity Verification where their real-life & project-related identity will be verified. 2017年第四季度：身份验证令牌启动持有人将可以要求身份验证，他们的现实生活与项目相关的身份将被验证。 IDV services will be paid in CRT by token holders. IDV服务将由CRT持有人支付。 As a gesture to the Open Source community & to increase the value of the initial CONTRIB network long-time Open Source contributors will be offered free IDV in case of ICO overfunding.作为开源社区的姿态，为了提高初始CONTRIB网络的价值，长期以来的开放源代码贡献者将在ICO资金过剩的情况下提供免费的IDV。

Q1 2018 : Contrib Platform Launch The scope of the initial CONTRIB platform has been kept small on purpose & continuous development will mainly consist of smart contracts that interact with the Contribution Graph. Q1 2018：的Contrib平台推出初期的contrib平台的范围一直保持在目的和持续发展小会主要由与贡献图形交互智能合同。 Examples of potential applications are outlined in the “Further Research” section.潜在应用的例子在“进一步研究”部分中概述。

Public ICO公共ICO

The Public ICO will start on July 14 2017. The ICO goal is to raise 1M USD, equivalent to a small startup seed-round.公共ICO将于2017年7月14日开始。ICO的目标是提高1M美元，相当于一个小型的初创企业。 The ticker symbol will be CRT and the coin will be a standard ECR20 token.股票代号将是CRT，硬币将是标准的ECR20标记。

The token is a utility token that people will use in the network to pay for initial identity verification and vote on project and role descriptions.令牌是人们在网络中使用的实用令牌，用于支付初始身份验证并对项目和角色描述进行投票。

The token will be tradable immediately after the ICO ends and usable in the CONTRIB network in Q4 2017.令牌将在ICO结束后立即交易，并可在2017年第四季度在CONTRIB网络中使用。
